# Third-Party Vendor Risk Assessment

This project demonstrates a practical vendor risk workflow, including a weighted questionnaire, evidence requests, scoring logic, and a sample assessment outcome for a critical cloud vendor.

## Files
- `Third_Party_Risk_Methodology.docx`
- `Third_Party_Risk_Methodology.pdf`
- `Vendor_Security_Questionnaire_and_Assessment.xlsx`

## What this demonstrates
- Third-party risk management
- Questionnaire design
- Weighted scoring methodology
- Conditional approval decision-making
